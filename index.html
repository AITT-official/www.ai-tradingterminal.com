About AITT

AITT (AI Trading Terminal) is an innovative trading platform that combines the power of artificial intelligence with offline-first architecture. Our mission is to provide traders with intelligent insights and tools that work reliably, regardless of network conditions.

Built with modern web technologies and progressive web app capabilities, AITT ensures that you can make informed trading decisions anytime, anywhere.
